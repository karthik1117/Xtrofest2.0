  • Registration
  • Event day

Steps:

  1. The Team leader of each team are requested to register for the event using the link below.
  2. It'll be a team registration. Hence, the team members are not required to register individually.
  3. Based on the solution proposed by the teams for the given problem statement, the teams will be shortlisted.
  4. The solution can be submitted as a simple text document or as a presentation or any other necessary format.
  5. The solution must be uploaded in your registration form.
  6. Our team will directly contact the shortlisted teams after the selection process.
  7. For further details about the event day, kindly check the next tab.

Problem Statement:

Virtual schools in rural areas

Statement Explanation:

Due to this pandemic and subsequent lock down educational institutions have been closed all around the country. Online classes are used to continue teaching. But not all students have access to laptops and good network. This puts them in an disadvantage compared to urban students.

Identify the various problems faced by the rural students with regards to online education and provide your solutions for the same.

Instructions

  1. The shortlisted teams will be provided with the problem statements on the event day.
  2. The Team must develop a solution within the span of the event.#24-Hours
  3. The solution is strictly instructed to be web based.
  4. As it's a virtual event, hardware solution are prohibited.
  5. The tech stacks which can be used by the teams are provided below.
